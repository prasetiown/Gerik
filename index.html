Supported Gestures

Right Hand

Desktop control and interaction

Left Click

Pinch thumb and index finger of right hand

Drag

Pinch thumb and index finger of right hand, hold, move hands

Right Click

Pinch thumb and middle finger of right hand

Mouse Scroll

Pinch thumb and ring finger of right hand, hold, move hands up and down

Enable/Disable Body Gesture

Pinch thumb and pinky finger of right hand and hold about 3 seconds

Left Hand

Space Button

Pinch thumb and index finger of left hand

Escape Button

Pinch thumb and middle finger of left hand

Middle Mouse Click

Pinch thumb and ring finger of left hand

Prepared for Next Feature

Pinch thumb and pinky finger of left hand are still do nothing
